Data Collection

We collect information that helps us provide a premium experience. This includes:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and studio preferences when you register.
  • Usage Data: Which assets you download and how you interact with our search engine.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information to optimize asset delivery.

How We Use Data

Your data is used solely to enhance the Illurdraw ecosystem. We never sell your personal information to third parties.

  • To personalize your asset recommendations based on previous downloads.
  • To process transactions and provide customer support for your licenses.
  • To send critical updates regarding our design library and system changes.

Cookie Policy

We use high-performance cookies to remember your workspace settings and maintain your studio session. These small files allow us to provide a seamless experience without requiring constant re-authentication.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings, though some features of the Illurdraw Studio may be limited as a result.

Security Measures

We employ enterprise-grade encryption to protect your data. Our infrastructure is regularly audited to ensure that your creative assets and personal details remain confidential and secure against unauthorized access.

Your Rights

Under global data protection laws (including GDPR), you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. You may exercise these rights through your account settings or by contacting our privacy team.
